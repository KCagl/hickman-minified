Higher Education, Development & Training

Schools within an hour drive of Hickman County:

  • TCAT – Dickson
  • TCAT – Hohenwald
  • Columbia State Community College
  • Freed-Hardeman
  • MTSU – Satellite Campus on Columbia State Community College

Columbia State Community College and Fairview High School share a partnership for their Mechatronics program, where students are educated on technology that combines electronics and mechanical engineering. This partnership allows for students to be educated in the field then advance their knowledge to a collegiate level at Columbia State.

Hickman has two Tennessee College of Applied Technology (TCAT) in close vicinity. The top programs for TCAT Hohenwald and Dickson are:

  • Automotive Technology
  • Diesel Powered Equipment Technology
  • Machine Tool Technology
  • Mechatronics
  • Industrial Maintenance/Electricity
